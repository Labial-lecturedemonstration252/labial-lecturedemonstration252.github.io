💡 What is this app?

This software brings artificial intelligence to your desktop interface. It sits in your menu bar, which allows you to trigger a chat window whenever you need assistance. You no longer need to switch windows or keep a browser tab open to use ChatGPT. It remains hidden until you click the icon.

⚙️ System Requirements

Your computer must meet these basic standards to run the application:

  • Apple macOS version 12.0 or newer.
  • An active internet connection to communicate with the AI servers.
  • A valid ChatGPT account.
  • 50 megabytes of available disk space.

📥 Download and Installation

Follow these steps to get the app running on your machine:

  1. Visit the official project page to start the download.
  2. Locate the latest release file for your system.
  3. Open the downloaded package file.
  4. Drag the application icon into your Applications folder.
  5. Open the application from your Launchpad or Applications folder.

If you see a security warning on your first launch, visit your System Settings, navigate to Privacy & Security, and select "Open Anyway" to authorize the application.

🚀 How to use the app

Once you start the application, look for a small icon in your top menu bar. Clicking this icon reveals the chat interface. You can type your questions into the text box and press Enter to receive a response.

The app maintains your current chat context during your session. If you want to start a fresh conversation, click the reset button located in the menu window. You can also drag the corners of the window to change its size to suit your current task.

🛡️ Privacy and Data

This software acts as a bridge between your computer and the AI service. The app does not save your chat logs to your local hard drive. All interactions occur through an encrypted connection to ensure your conversations remain private. Ensure you use a strong password for your associated AI account.

🛠️ Troubleshooting

If the application does not open, ensure you have sufficient permissions to run apps from unknown developers. Check your network status if the chat window displays a connection error. Restarting the application usually resolves temporary software conflicts during the initial login process.
